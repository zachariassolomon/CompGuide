Patient Management

Patient profiles with all the applied protocols and procedures. This gives the health care professional a summary containing information about the patient state, procedures applied up until the moment of consultation, and the next steps in the clinical process.

Recommendation of Clinical Tasks

The execution engine behind CompGuide interprets the clinical protocol and runs it against information about the state of the patient, thus providing contextualized decision support regarding diagnostic activities or procedure recommendations.

Scheduling of Clinical Tasks

All tasks in the clinical protocol are mapped to the calendar of the health care professional in charge of them. This mapping respects the constraints about their relative order and their temporal restrictions.
